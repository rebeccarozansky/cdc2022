How do people Tweet about Climate Change?

CDC 2022 presentation



How did we get our data?

Using tweepy and the twitter API, we were able to collect more than 50,000 tweets tagged with climate change topics


  • #climatechange

  • #climatehoax

  • #climatechangeisreal

  • #globalwarming




What tweets generate the most traction?

  • - Using sentiment analysis we classified each tweet on a scale from (-1,1) to describe how negative or positive it was

  • - Tweets with a slightly positive sentiment value less than zero generated much more traction than their positive counterparts

  • - The more polarizing the tweet, the more attention it was likely to get

How does the weather affect someone’s tweets?

Using a weather API, we were able to correlate geo-tagged tweets with the user’s current weather


  • - As the temperature increased, the sentiment values of the tweets decreased

  • - This means that tweets were more negative as the weather got warmer

How do people tweet about climate change compared to everything else?

We examined the general sentiment of tweeting history of accounts that tweeted positively vs. negatively about climate change

  • - Accounts that tweeted positively about climate change had generally positive sentiment values in past tweets

  • - Accounts that tweeted negatively about climate change had generally negative sentiment values in past tweets

Which factors of a tweet help it gain popularity?

We analyzed different factors that increased the interaction with a tweet

  • - We found that a small amount of hashtags resulted in a large amount of retweets

  • - If a tweet contained too many hashtags, we see that the amount of retweets decreases drastically
